🧠 Challenge Description
-
Phantom Paradise

The original photographer of this picture commented the flag on his post.

“15 October, 2019 : a day I can't forget!”

The challenge hints that:

The image was posted online.

The original photographer left the flag in the comments.

The date is important: 15 October 2019.

🔎 Objective
-
Find the original source of the image and locate the photographer’s comment containing the flag.

🛠️ Methodology (Step-by-Step Solution)
-
1️⃣ Reverse Image Search
-
We started by performing a reverse image search using:

Google Images

TinEye

Yandex Image Search

This helps identify:

The original upload source

Social media posts

Related captions or comments

2️⃣ Narrowing Down by Date
-
The challenge specifically mentions:

📅 15 October, 2019
-
After locating potential matches, results were filtered by:

Upload date

Post history around that date

This significantly narrowed the search.

3️⃣ Identifying the Photographer
-
From the reverse image results:

The original photographer’s post was located.

The image matched the one provided in the challenge.

The post date matched 15 October 2019.

4️⃣ Checking the Comments Section
-
The hint clearly states:

“The original photographer commented the flag on his post.”

Upon reviewing the comments section, the photographer had posted a comment containing the flag.

🧩 Key Takeaways
-
Always perform reverse image searches in OSINT challenges.

Dates in descriptions are often critical filters.

Flags may be hidden in:

Comments

Captions

Metadata

Archived versions of pages

Always check the original source — not just reposts.

OSINT Investigation

🏁 Conclusion
-
This challenge demonstrates how small hints like a specific date can drastically reduce the search space in OSINT investigations.

The flag was hidden in plain sight — inside the photographer’s own comment.
